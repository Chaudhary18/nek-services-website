Common questions

Answers before you book the call.

The questions we hear most from US businesses and CPA firms before getting started.

Do you work with businesses that are months or years behind on their books-

Yes - this is one of our most common starting points. We catch up backlogs of any length, fix the errors, reconcile the accounts, and get your books to a clean baseline. Most clients are caught up within the first month of engagement.

Which accounting software do you support-

We regularly work with QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, Xero, Wave, Zoho Books, FreshBooks, Aplos, and Excel. We also support business platforms including Shopify, Square, Toast POS, Housecall Pro, PayPal, and others. If you use something not on this list, ask us - we adapt quickly.

What does CFO-style advisory mean for a small business-

It means you get financial guidance that goes beyond bookkeeping. Ratio analysis, profitability review, cash flow planning, and reporting that supports real decisions. You don't need a full-time CFO to have someone review your numbers strategically every month.

For CPA firms: do you communicate directly with our clients-

Only if that is what your firm wants. By default, all communication stays within your firm's process. We support the work quietly, under your brand and your confidentiality rules. Client ownership stays with you - always.

How do you protect client confidentiality-

Every engagement operates under a strict NDA and confidentiality agreement. We follow your firm's communication channels, use approved software, and never share client data outside the agreed scope. Confidentiality is a core operating principle at NEK.

What happens after I book a free call-

Within 24 hours of booking, Muhammad Ilyas will personally reach out to confirm the time and ask a few short questions so the call is useful from the first minute. No sales pressure - just a direct conversation about your books, your needs, and whether NEK is the right fit.
