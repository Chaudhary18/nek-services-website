Your questions

What firm owners ask before getting started.

Direct answers to the questions that matter most when evaluating an outsourced accounting partner.

Will our clients know you are involved-

No - unless you choose to tell them. All work is delivered under your firm's name. We have no direct contact with your clients unless you explicitly instruct us to. Your client relationship, your brand, your confidentiality standard.

How do you protect our clients' confidential financial data-

Every engagement begins with a signed NDA. We work only within the communication channels and software platforms you approve. Client data is never shared outside your agreed scope. Our team members are trained on data handling protocols and we do not subcontract your work to any third party.

Which accounting and tax software do you work in-

For bookkeeping: QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, Xero, Wave, Zoho Books, FreshBooks, Aplos, and Excel. For tax preparation: Drake, TaxSlayer, and TaxWise. We also adapt to your practice management system and document workflow. If you use something not listed, ask us.

How do you handle volume spikes during busy season-

This is exactly where outsourcing pays off. Because we are not full-time staff, you can scale the volume of work we handle up during busy periods and down during slower months. We plan capacity with you in advance so your deadlines are never at risk.

What is your typical turnaround time-

Turnaround depends on scope and volume, which we confirm before every engagement begins. For regular monthly bookkeeping, we work to your firm's close schedule. For tax preparation support, we work to your filing deadlines. If we cannot meet a specific requirement, we say so before starting - not after.
